Making White Rice

  1. Combine 1 cup of rice with 2 cups of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting.
  3. Cook for about 18 minutes.

Sprouted California Rice

Combine 1 1/4 cups of rice with 2 cups of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

For slightly al dente rice: Combine 1 1/4 cups of rice with 2 cups of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.
